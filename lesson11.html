Lesson 11Research Design
Ms. Ishadi Nilaweera

This topic was about what research is and how to do it well. Research means finding new ideas or using old ones in new ways to solve problems. It's done step by step by studying, collecting info, and thinking carefully. We must use facts, not just guess. We should be honest and not copy others work. A research paper has parts like title, authors, abstract, intro, methods, results, and conclusion. Title tells what the paper is about. Intro explains background and why topic matters. Literature review shows what others said. Methods say how we did study, who we asked, and tools used. Then results and their meaning. At end, conclusion and references. Abstract is a short summary at the start.
