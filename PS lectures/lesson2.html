Lesson 02Portfolio management

Portfolios are used to show skills, work, and achievements. In jobs, they help prove your ability and support promotions. Creative workers like artists and writers use them to show style and past work. Students use them to track progress, and teachers use them to show growth. Freelancers attract clients with past projects. They also include training, certificates, and new skills. Portfolios help in reviews, career changes, networking, and business pitches. Types include personal, professional, job-focused, and learning portfolios. Sites like LinkedIn, Behance, and WordPress make it easy to create and share portfolios online.
