Lesson 04CV & Cover letter Writing
Ms. Ishara Weerasinghe

A CV (Curriculum Vitae) is a important tool when applying for jobs. It show your education, work experience, skills, and what you achieved. This lesson tell us how to make a clear and professional CV that give good first impression to employer. It also teach the basics of cover letter a small document that go with your CV to say why you fit for the job. A good CV and cover letter can help you get noticed and maybe get more interview chances.
