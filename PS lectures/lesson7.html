Lesson 07Email Etiquettes
Ms. Ishara Weerasinghe

This lesson was about why online etiquette is important when we go to work, college or internships. The way we talk online, like in emails, really affect how people see us. If we write bad message or post something careless, it can hurt our chances. But if we act nice and write properly, it shows we are professional and can be trusted. This lesson mostly talked about how to write good emails and what mistakes to not do.
